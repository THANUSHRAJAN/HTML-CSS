KOHINOOR DIAMOND

The most expensive diamond in the world, the Kohinoor has been given a priceless status due to its uniqueness and brilliance. The diamond is 105.6 carats and is placed right on top of the UK sovereign's crown.

PINK DIAMOND

Pink diamonds often feature secondary hues - an additional modifying color. The most common modifying colors are orange, brown and purple. These diamonds would be described in the certificate as orange pink, brown pink and purple pink.

BLUE DIAMOND

They are colored blue by trace amounts of boron that contaminate the crystalline lattice structure. Blue diamonds belong to a subcategory of diamonds called fancy color diamonds, the generic name for diamonds that exhibit intense color.

RED DIAMOND

A red diamond is a diamond which displays red colour and exhibits the same mineral properties as colourless diamonds. Red diamonds are commonly known as the most expensive and the rarest diamond colour in the world.
